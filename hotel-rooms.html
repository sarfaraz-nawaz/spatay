About This Hotel

Overlooking the Indian Ocean, this posh cliff-top resort is 6 km from the Pura Luhur Uluwatu temple and 9 km from the Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park.
Refined 1- and 2-bedroom villas feature ocean views, free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minibars, plus coffeemakers, private gardens and plunge pools. An upgraded villa adds a screening room and an infinity pool. Luxe mansions have 3 to 5 bedrooms. Butler service is available 24/7.

Breakfast is included. Other amenities consist of a bar with ocean views, and 3 chic restaurants, including an open-air Italian option. There's an outdoor pool, a spa and a gym, as well as a private beach.
